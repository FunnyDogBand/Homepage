About the Band

Funny Dog is an alternative rock band from OKC and Norman blending 90s nostalgia with a modern garage edge. Formed by friends with a shared love of fuzz pedals and punchy hooks, the band delivers high-energy performances and introspective lyrics. From local venues to basement shows, Funny Dog has been steadily building a loyal following of fans who howl back.
